🎯 What is SRM Games Hub?

SRM Games Hub is a collection of 10 interactive games designed to help sales, service, and business professionals master the complete business pipeline through engaging gameplay.

🎮 How It Works

  • Game 1: Business Pipeline - Arrange 21 levels of business processes
  • Game 2-10: Coming soon - Each focusing on a specific business area
  • Each game teaches structured, sequential thinking
  • Perfect for team training and competitions

🏆 The Business Pipeline Game

  • 21 Levels covering complete business process
  • 4 Teams can compete simultaneously
  • 9 Steps per level to arrange in correct order
  • 60-second timer adds urgency
  • Brain Stormer hints for thinking prompts
  • Wrong answer restarts from Level 1
  • Score tracking across all levels

📊 Business Processes Covered

  • Lead Generation → Qualification → Quotation → Negotiation → Finalisation
  • Drawings → Site Preparation → Payment → Delivery → Handover
  • Free Service → AMC Quotation → AMC Negotiation → AMC Payment
  • Modernisation → Upgradation → Replacement → AMC Strategy

  • Perfect for team building and training
  • Track your progress across all games
  • Compete with colleagues and learn together
